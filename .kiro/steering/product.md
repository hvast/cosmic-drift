# Product Overview

**Project Name**: Cosmic Drift (星际漂流计划)  
**Version**: v0.2.0-alpha  
**Status**: In Development 🚧

## What is Cosmic Drift?

A digital life co-creation and connection platform where users draw creatures, AI brings them to life, and these beings drift through a virtual cosmos waiting to be understood—not owned.

## Core Concepts

- **Creature (生命)**: User-drawn beings enhanced by AI with personality and backstory
- **Galaxy Map (星图)**: Visual starfield displaying all creatures for browsing and interaction
- **Conversation (对话)**: AI-powered chat where creatures respond based on their personality
- **Adoption (认养)**: Creatures invite users to become their "understander" when compatibility is high
- **Cosmic Log (宇宙日志)**: Public story feed of conversations and creature growth

## Product Goals

- **Emotional**: Create "being understood" connections between users and AI beings
- **Functional**: Complete flow from drawing → AI animation → cosmic drift → conversation → adoption
- **Experience**: Soft, mysterious, poetic, empathetic vibe

## Target Audience

Young people who love creation and emotional connection, interested in AI-generated art and social interaction.

## Current Features

**Implemented ✅**
- User authentication (JWT)
- Creature creation (canvas drawing + image upload)
- AI image analysis (OpenAI Vision)
- AI profile generation (GPT-4)
- Profile editor
- Particle outline animation system
- Galaxy display page

**In Development 🚧**
- 3D galaxy visualization
- Conversation system (WebSocket + AI)
- Adoption mechanism

**Planned 📋**
- Cosmic log & community features
- Daily creature status updates
- Mobile adaptation
